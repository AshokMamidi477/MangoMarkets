# Mango Markets — Product Philosophy

## Core Identity
Mango Markets is an **AI-first market intelligence app**, not a price tracker.
Users open it to **understand markets**, not just check prices.

## The Golden Rule
**Data → AI → UI** (always)
Never: UI → data → AI

Every screen, every feature must answer: "Where is AI in this?"
If you can't answer — don't build it yet.

## 5-Second Test
When a user opens the app, within 5 seconds they must see:
**"What's happening in markets?"** — an AI-generated insight, not raw numbers.

## AI-First Principles
- AI insights come BEFORE raw data on every screen
- Every data point should have an AI interpretation layer
- Popups should lead with AI analysis, not just field/value pairs
- The app thinks in narratives: "Markets are bearish due to Fed signals", "Crypto outperforming tech stocks today"
- Raw prices are supporting evidence for AI conclusions, not the main content

## Feature Alignment
Every feature must feed AI or be fed by AI:
| Feature | AI Role |
|---------|---------|
| Dashboard | AI summary is the hero, data supports it |
| Stocks/Crypto segments | Feed AI with context for insights |
| Detail Popup | Quick AI insight + supporting data |
| Portfolio | AI analysis of performance, not just numbers |
| Watchlist | AI explains why movers are moving |
| Sentiment | AI interprets, not just shows a score |
| Heatmap | AI explains the pattern |

## What Makes This App Unique
Users don't open it to: check price ❌
Users open it to: understand market ✅

## Danger Zone
The easy path is a UI-heavy app. The hard path is an AI-first product.
Always choose the hard path.
